Mostafa Abdelmeguid

MA candidate in the Creative Cultural Enterpreneurship programmer at Goldsmiths, University of London. He is focusing on the revival of the historical neighborhood of Darb El Labana in Historic Cairo, Egypt, hoping for it to be a hub for reviving vernacular architecture, craftmanship, aesthetics, and create both creative and cultural opportunities in which will benefit the local community.
